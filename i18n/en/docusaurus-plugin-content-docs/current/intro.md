## 📌 What it is {#what-it-is}

- **Shittim Canvas NEXT** is a Unity-based **showcase** app: use it as a **desktop wallpaper** or a **desk pet**. It focuses on recreating *Blue Archive*–style flat animated scenes—**Memorial Lobby**, **story live backgrounds**, playback, and presentation.

- The goal: match in-game visuals, animation logic, and interaction as closely as possible—then go further with extensibility and customization.

## ✨ Highlights {#highlights}

- ### 🎮 Faithful recreation
    - Memorial Lobby and story live backgrounds, visually
    - Particles, post-processing, and the rest of the eye candy
    - Animation blending and full playback logic
    - Interaction logic that mirrors the game
    - Ambient and intro audio where it matters

- ### 🛠️ Room to customize
    - Planned: import and tweak **Spine 4.2** Memorial Lobby assets
    - Planned: more unofficial customization features
    - Planned: extend official Memorial Lobby interaction patterns
    - Planned: user-defined interaction regions
